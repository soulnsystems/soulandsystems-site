Gratitude Journal

Pause for a moment before writing, look back and find proud or happy moments

• For the past day what am I happy/proud that happened?
• For the past week what brought joy to my life?
• What is one good thing you did for the past month?

One sentence can change a lot of things.

If there is nothing also it's alright, ask yourself at least one of these questions tommorrow
