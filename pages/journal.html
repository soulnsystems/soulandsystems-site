Awareness Journal

Full content of the Mindfulness journal goes here...

Pause for a moment before writing.

• What feels present in me right now?
• Where do I feel tension — body or mind?
• What am I avoiding noticing?

Write without fixing, whatever answer comes to your mind. No structure, just write your mind out.

Honesty with yourself counts.
— Soul and Systems

Gratitude Journal

Pause for a moment before writing, look back and find proud or happy moments

• For the past day what am I happy/proud that happened?
• For the past week what brought joy to my life?
• What is one good thing you did for the past month?

One sentence can change a lot of things.

If there is nothing also it's alright, ask yourself at least one of these questions tommorrow
— Soul and Systems

Self-Evaluation

Choose one thing you've been procrastinating out of fear.

• Break it down into 15 minutes starter time. (ex. write the email line, start the design)
• Set a time and just start, no editing just begin.

Write slowly. One sentence is enough.

Soft resets count too. No pressure, just presence.
— Soul and Systems

Starting Over

• What am I leaving behind?

• What kind of beginning do I need right now?

Start where you are.

One step ahead of the other.

Bonus Space

Write down one powerful "I am" statement that contradicts the critic (I am qualified, I am growing, I derserve this seat)

• Repeat it for a week looking at yourself in the mirror.
• Bonus : Record it as a voice note and listen to it daily.
• Pause. Breathe. Celebrate smallness.

Only you can do what it takes to get there.
— Soul and Systems
