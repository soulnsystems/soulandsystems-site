Evaluation Exercise

🧠 Think

Choose one thing you’ve been procrastinating out of fear.

🗂️ Plan

Break it down into a 15-minute starter task (e.g., write the email line, design the page).

⚡ Act

Set a time and just start. No editing. Just begin.

Soft resets count too. No pressure — just one step at a time.
